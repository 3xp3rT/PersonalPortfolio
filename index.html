How We Engage

We have a team of business analysts with expertise in manufacturing, retail, healthcare, banking and financial services, following the latest company, technology and marketing trends., A dedicated BA or BAs team must translate the concept into comprehensive criteria and specifications, either on their own or in conjunction with your BAs. To ensure the high quality of our solutions as well as delivering applications within agreed time and budget constraints, we have been involved from the very beginning with our QA team in the project. They study requirements and user stories, design architecture, compliance with technology, and continue with test planning.

Share with our team your specific expectations and we will do the rest to make your dream a ready-to-go solution.
Development Testing Deployment and set-up After-release support
We develop your solution's first working version in 3-9 months (depending on the complexity of its functionality) by using Agile methodologies. And after that, in regular iterations, we continue to scale then improve your apps.
We check that your program is running as expected and that it meets all the requirements set and that your data is safe. We use test automation wherever possible to reduce testing time and costs.
In your IT environment, we get the software running, train your support team if necessary, and teach the employees how to use it.
We respond to your post-release questions for 1-3 months, handle incidents, changes in configuration, and updates.
Upon agreement, we will assume responsibility for long-term maintenance and support of applications and continue to take care of application performance, reliability enhancement and development after the warranty service is over.
